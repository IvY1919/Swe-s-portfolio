About ME!

Hi!
I'm Swe Zin Phyu.

I am originally from Yangon, Myanmar, and currently residing in Japan. Over the past four years, I have been working in the automobile industry, as an Embedded System Engineer. After completing my studies at the University of Information Technology (UIT) in December 2019,I embarked on a journey to Japan and joined the Embedded System Department of Nippon Seki Corporation. Whith three years of experience in this field, I have honed my skills and experties in developing embedded systems within the automotive sector.

Currently, I am working as a Web Developer, specializing in EC site design and management using WordPress. My focus is on creating user-friendly, visually appealing websites that enhance online shopping experiences. I have always been passionate about technology and design. My transition from embedded systems to web development was driven by my deep interest in crafting digital experiences. I enjoy working with WordPress, front-end technologies, and design tools to build engaging and functional websites.

Committed to become a web development career, I invest my time in mastering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Java. Continuous learning strengthens my ability to create engaging web experiences. Exploring WordPress and Figma broadens my skill set, enhancing my proficiency in user-friendly and visually captivating websites.

I have developed this portfolio website with the aim of showcasing my present skill set and past projects, with the ultimate goal of securing my first position as a web developer.
