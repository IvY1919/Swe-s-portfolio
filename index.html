About ME!

Hi!
I'm Swe Zin Phyu.

I am originally from Yangon, Myanmar, and currently residing in Japan. Over the past three years, I have been working in the automobile industry, as an Embedded System Engineer. After completing my studies at the University of Information Technology (UIT) in December 2019,I embarked on a journey to Japan and joined the Embedded System Department of Nippon Seki Corporation. Whith three years of experience in this field, I have honed my skills and experties in developing embedded systems within the automotive sector.

However, During my time in the automobile industry, I discovered a profound passion and talent for web designing and development. The process of creating, drawing, and designing has continually captured my imagination and motivated me to explore a career path as a front-end developer. I am fueled by the opportunity to bring captivating digital experiences to life through visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces. With a keen eye for detail and a dedication to delivering exceptional user experiences, I am eager to contribute my expertise and pursue a fulfilling career in the world of web development.

Committed to become a front-end development career, I invest my time in mastering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. Continuous learning strengthens my ability to create engaging web experiences. Exploring WordPress and Figma broadens my skill set, enhancing my proficiency in user-friendly and visually captivating websites.

I have developed this portfolio website with the aim of showcasing my present skill set and past projects, with the ultimate goal of securing my first position as a junior web developer.
